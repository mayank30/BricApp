About Us

In a world evolving with digitization, usage of business cards is still prevalent as a means of exchanging professional identity. This entails that business cards are carried always and the cards received are stored in a folder which also needs to be manually transposed to a phone or email as a contact. This is a laborious process and is there a better way of doing this whole operation in seconds ??

Here comes Bric !!

Bric is a mobile app that digitizes business cards and lets you not only store the cards of your contacts but also enables to share your own card from your mobile phone to your contacts. And whats more ? Each time your contact gets promoted, changes location or jobs, you automatically get the updated contact information along with the new business card.

Say good bye to paper. Say hello to Bric ! !
