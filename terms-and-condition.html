2. Online / Mobile Application Platform

Bric is a mobile app that digitizes business cards and lets you not only store the cards of your contacts but also enables to share your own card from your mobile phone to your contacts. The Applicaiton scans physical business cards through the device camera of a mobile phone and converts image into readable text / data and stores it in a digital form. This data along with the digitized copy of the business card can be used by users to call, sms, email and exchange business cards. Bric provides the originality and visual appeal of a physical card and at the same time delivers all the utility derived from a business card over a touch, tap or swipe on the phone.Each time your contact gets promoted, changes location or jobs, you automatically get the updated contact information along with the new business card
