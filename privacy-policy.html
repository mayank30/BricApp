The Mobile App is a platform where by it scans physical business cards through the device camera of a mobile phone and converts image into readable text  data and stores it in a digital form. This data along with the digitized copy of the business card can be used by users to call, sms, email and exchange business cards. Bric provides the originality and visual appeal of a physical card and at the same time delivers all the utility derived from a business card over a touch, tap or swipe on the phone. Bric helps the user to store their contacts, share their card, remain updated with their contacts
