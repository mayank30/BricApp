FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I save a contact in Bric?
To save a contact click on the + button in the home screen and focus the camera on the card and click.

What is the right way to scan and save a card?

a) Place the card on a table and ensure there is adequate lighting

b) Avoid shiny & transparent back grounds

c) Ensure there is no shadow or dim lights & use flash if required

d) Focus the camera on the card till the image is clear and then click

e) If the image box does not auto fit, drag and reposition the image box to the outline of the card

f) If the data does not read accurately, try again for optimum results

g) Edit the fields if necessary if some letters are not read properly

Am I required to save my card to use Bric?

While it is not mandatory to have or save your card to use Bric, however you may do so to exchange your cards seamlessly with your contacts.

How do I update my card?

Go to the my card option and click on the edit option in your card profile, from here you can replace your card or update your contact information

What happens when I update my card?

All your contacts with whom you have shared your card through Bric will automatically get your new contact details

How do I share my card with a contact?

You can go to the “My card “option on your app and first save your card. Once you save your car, you can swipe up to share your card with your contacts

Do my contacts need to be on Bric to receive my card?

Yes, however if they are not on Bric you can send your card through an SMS link and they will receive your card as soon as they download the app

Is my personal data secure with Bric?

Bric follows highest levels of security & confidentiality and you may refer to our terms of service & privacy policy for further details

How do I update my registered details?

a) Go to the settings menu by clicking on the icon on the top right corner of the home screen.

b) Select Account Info  Personal Information

c) You can update your Name, email id & Phone No.

d) Handle/Username is NOT editable as its your unique identity on the BRIC network.

e) Click on save/tick icon.

How do I invite my friends and colleagues to download BRIC?

a) Go to the settings menu by clicking on the icon on the top right corner of the home screen

b) Select ‘Invite to download’ option

c) It opens up your SMS window with pre populated download information

d) Enter one or more mobile nos. to send the SMS

e) NOTE: We will not charge you but SMS charges will be applicable from your service provider as per your billing plan.

How do I highlight an issue or provide feed back?

You can write to us at support@bricapp.com
